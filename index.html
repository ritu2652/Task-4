About Us

At Teela, we've redefined the art of outdoor adventure and luxury living. Nestled in the heart of nature's embrace, our glamping resort offers a haven of serenity and indulgence, where the wild meets the refined. We believe that the untamed wilderness and creature comforts can coexist harmoniously, providing an experience that is both exhilarating and opulent. Our accommodations are an embodiment of rustic elegance.
